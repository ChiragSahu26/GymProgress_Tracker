The Push Pull Leg Split

What is it?

    The Push Pull Legs split divides training into three categories: pushing movements (chest, shoulders, triceps), 
    pulling movements (back, biceps), and legs. It is one of the most popular splits for both strength and hypertrophy.

Who is it for?

  • Intermediate and advanced lifters
  • People training 5–6 days per week
  • People training 5–6 days per week
  • Those who enjoy higher training frequency

Pros

  • High training frequency
  • Excellent muscle growth potential
  • Flexible exercise selection
  • Easy progression tracking

Cons

  • Requires frequent gym visits
  • Leg days can be demanding
  • Recovery may become challenging

Recovery Requirements

    Moderate to high. Sleep, nutrition, and fatigue management are important.
